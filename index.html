mission

Bridging the gender gap in Tech one free workshop at a time! We host fun and friendly workshops for young students to learn programming at schools and universities local to them. Through our unique approach, and our intuitive curriculum, we plan to give rise to a new generation of female programmers set to take the world by storm!




confidence

Instill confidence in young girls to stand tall and unapologetically in an industry dominated by men.

community

Build a tight-knit community of young female programmers to support, motivate, and inspire each other.

curiosity

Inspire students to seek more knowledge, to question everything, and to build on their curiosity for Computer Science.
